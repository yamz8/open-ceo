# Example: Good Month Update

A sample investor update for a strong performance month.

---

**Subject: Acme Corp Monthly Update - January 2024**

Hi everyone,

**TL;DR:**
- Hit $2M ARR milestone (+15% MoM) - ahead of plan
- Closed Globex as our largest customer ever ($150K ACV)
- Launched self-serve tier - 23 signups in first week

---

## Highlights

**Revenue milestone**
We crossed $2M ARR this month, about 6 weeks ahead of plan. Growth driven by strong enterprise performance - we closed 4 deals totaling $280K in new ARR.

**Globex deal closed**
Our largest deal ever: $150K ACV with Globex Industries. 18-month contract with expansion potential to $300K. Thanks to [Board Member] for the intro - made a huge difference.

**Self-serve launch**
Launched our self-serve tier on Jan 15. Early results encouraging:
- 23 paid signups in first 2 weeks
- $2K MRR already (small but growing)
- 4.2 average rating in onboarding survey

**Team growth**
Sarah Kim joined as VP Marketing from Stripe. Already making an impact on our positioning and demand gen strategy.

## Challenges

**Enterprise sales cycle lengthening**
Seeing cycles extend from 8 to 12 weeks in enterprise. Two factors: (1) tighter budgets requiring more approvals, (2) our POC process is too long.

We're addressing this by:
- Adding ROI calculator to shorten evaluation
- Hiring solutions engineer to accelerate POCs
- Creating "quick start" package for faster time-to-value

## Asks

1. **Intro to Initech** - They're in our sweet spot (Series B fintech, 200 employees). [Board Member], I think you know their CFO?

2. **VP Sales candidates** - Starting search next month. Would love referrals for people who've built enterprise motions at similar stage.

3. **Pricing feedback** - Considering 15% price increase for enterprise tier. Any pattern matching on timing/approach?

[CEO Name]

---

*Why this works:*
- Leads with specific, quantified wins
- TL;DR captures the key points
- Metrics are scannable
- Challenge is honest with action plan
- Asks are specific and actionable
- Appropriate length (~400 words)
