# Engineering Compensation Guide

## Engineering Levels

### Level Definitions

| Level | Title Examples | Experience | Scope |
|-------|---------------|------------|-------|
| IC1 | Junior Engineer | 0-2 years | Tasks with guidance |
| IC2 | Engineer | 2-4 years | Features independently |
| IC3 | Senior Engineer | 4-7 years | Projects, mentors others |
| IC4 | Staff Engineer | 7-10+ years | Multi-team impact |
| IC5 | Principal Engineer | 10+ years | Org-wide architecture |
| M1 | Engineering Manager | 5+ years | Manages 3-8 engineers |
| M2 | Senior EM / Director | 8+ years | Manages managers |
| M3 | VP Engineering | 10+ years | Engineering org leader |

## Specialty Premiums

Certain specializations command premium compensation:

| Specialty | Salary Premium | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Machine Learning / AI | +15-25% | High demand, scarce supply |
| Security | +10-20% | Critical expertise |
| Infrastructure / SRE | +10-15% | Scale expertise |
| Mobile (iOS/Android) | +5-10% | Platform-specific skills |
| Frontend | Baseline | Most common |
| Backend | Baseline | Most common |
| Full-stack | Baseline to +5% | Versatility value |
| Data Engineering | +10-15% | Growing demand |

## Seed Stage Engineering

### Typical Team Structure
- 2-5 engineers
- Often no formal levels
- Generalists preferred
- Everyone wears multiple hats

### Compensation Philosophy
- Heavier equity weighting
- Below-market base acceptable
- Mission and ownership emphasis
- First 5 engineers: 0.5-2% each is common

### Seed Engineering Ranges

| Role | Base Salary | Equity |
|------|-------------|--------|
| First engineer | $100-140K | 1.0-2.0% |
| Engineer #2-5 | $110-150K | 0.25-1.0% |
| Senior hire | $130-170K | 0.25-0.75% |

## Series A Engineering

### Typical Team Structure
- 8-20 engineers
- Start formalizing levels
- Tech lead roles emerge
- May hire first EM

### Compensation Philosophy
- More competitive base
- Still equity-forward
- Attract experienced talent
- Balance generalists + specialists

### Series A Engineering Ranges

| Role | Base Salary | Equity |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Junior (IC1) | $80-115K | 0.01-0.05% |
| Mid (IC2) | $100-150K | 0.025-0.10% |
| Senior (IC3) | $140-190K | 0.05-0.30% |
| Staff (IC4) | $170-230K | 0.15-0.50% |
| EM | $150-200K | 0.10-0.35% |
| Director | $180-250K | 0.25-0.75% |

## Series B+ Engineering

### Typical Team Structure
- 25-100+ engineers
- Clear level framework
- Multiple teams/pods
- Engineering leadership layer

### Compensation Philosophy
- Competitive with big tech
- Structured comp bands
- Refresh grants for retention
- Performance-based adjustments

## Engineering Interview Assessment

### Technical Skills to Assess
- System design ability
- Code quality and testing
- Debugging and problem-solving
- Technical communication
- Architecture decisions

### Behavioral Competencies
- Ownership and initiative
- Collaboration
- Handling ambiguity
- Learning velocity
- Technical judgment

## Making Competitive Offers

### When Competing with Big Tech
- Highlight equity upside story
- Emphasize scope and impact
- Show career growth path
- Consider signing bonus

### When Budget-Constrained
- Lead with mission and vision
- Offer more equity
- Flexible working arrangements
- Unique learning opportunities
- Title (if meaningful)
