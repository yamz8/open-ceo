# Executive Compensation Guide

## Executive Roles Overview

Executive compensation is highly variable based on company stage, funding, experience, and negotiation. These ranges are guidelines.

## C-Level Compensation

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

| Stage | Base Salary | Equity |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Seed (co-founder) | $80-120K | 5-15% |
| Seed (hired) | $120-180K | 1-3% |
| Series A | $180-280K | 0.75-2.0% |
| Series B | $250-350K | 0.5-1.25% |
| Series C+ | $300-450K | 0.25-0.75% |

**Key considerations:**
- Co-founder CTO typically has significant equity from founding
- Hired CTO at later stages focuses on scaling, different skill set
- Technical credibility matters more than people management at seed

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

| Stage | Base Salary | Equity |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Series A | $200-300K | 0.5-1.5% |
| Series B | $280-380K | 0.3-0.8% |
| Series C+ | $350-500K | 0.2-0.5% |

**Key considerations:**
- Rarely needed before Series A (use fractional CFO)
- Critical for fundraising, M&A, potential IPO
- Public company CFO experience commands premium

### Chief Operating Officer (COO)

| Stage | Base Salary | Equity |

**Key considerations:**

### Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

### Chief Revenue Officer (CRO) / Chief Sales Officer

| Stage | Base | OTE | Equity |
|-------|------|-----|--------|
| Series A | $200-280K | $350-500K | 0.75-2.0% |
| Series B | $250-350K | $450-650K | 0.4-1.0% |

**Key considerations:**

### Chief People Officer (CPO) / Head of People

| Stage | Base Salary | Equity |
|-------|-------------|--------|

| Series B | $230-320K | 0.2-0.5% |

## Executive Package Components

### Base Salary
- Paid regularly (semi-monthly or bi-weekly)
- Executives often below market cash, premium in equity
- CEO sometimes takes $0 or minimal salary at seed

### Annual Bonus
- Typically 20-50% of base for executives
- Tied to company and individual performance
- May be discretionary at early stages

### Equity
- Usually 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
- Some executives negotiate 3-year vesting
- May include acceleration on change of control

### Change of Control Provisions
- Single trigger: Acceleration on acquisition
- Double trigger: Acceleration on acquisition + termination
- Double trigger more common, investor-friendly

### Severance
- Executives may negotiate severance packages
- Typical: 6-12 months base on termination without cause
- May include benefit continuation

## Hiring Executive Talent

### When to Hire
- VP Engineering: When eng team reaches 10-15 engineers
- VP Sales: When you have repeatable sales motion
- VP Product: When product complexity requires senior leadership
- CFO: When approaching Series B or complex financials
- COO: When CEO is bottleneck on operations

### What to Look For
- Stage-appropriate experience
- Ability to scale (not just operate)
- Cultural fit with existing team
- References from investors and peers
- Track record building teams, not just joining them

### Red Flags
- Only big company experience
- Can't articulate what they specifically did
- Wants to hire their entire team immediately
- Uncomfortable with startup ambiguity
- Primarily motivated by title

| Seed | $0-10K | 0.25-0.5% |
| Series A | $10-25K | 0.15-0.25% |

| Series C+ | $40-75K | 0.05-0.15% |
