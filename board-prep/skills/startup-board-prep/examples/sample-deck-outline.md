## Formatting Notes

### Slide Design
- Use company brand colors consistently
- One key message per slide
- Maximum 6 bullet points
- Include source/date on data slides

### Charts
- Show trends (6-12 months history)
- Use consistent axis scales
- Label directly (minimize legends)
- Highlight current period

### Headlines
- Headlines should convey the takeaway
- Not just "Financial Update" but "Revenue exceeded plan by 15%"
