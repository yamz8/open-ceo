# Stakeholder Communication Templates

## Board Communication

### Emergency Board Update

**When to use:** Major crisis requiring immediate board awareness

**Subject:** [Company] - Urgent Update: [Brief Description]

> Dear Board,
>
> I'm writing to inform you of a developing situation that requires your awareness.
>
> **Situation:**
> [2-3 sentences describing what happened]
>
> **Current Status:**
> [What's happening right now]
>
> **Our Response:**
> [What you're doing about it]
>
> **What We Need:**
> - [Decision/approval needed, if any]
> - [Support or resources needed]
> - [FYI only, if no action needed]
>
> **Next Steps:**
> [Timeline and planned actions]
>
> I'm available for a call at [times]. Please let me know if you'd like to discuss.
>
> [Name]

### Layoff Notification to Board

**When to use:** Before or immediately after layoff execution

**Subject:** [Company] - Workforce Reduction Update

> Dear Board,
>
> I want to inform you that we [will be / have] conducted a reduction in force today.
>
> **Summary:**
> - Reducing team from [X] to [Y] ([Z]% reduction)
> - Effective [date]
> - All affected employees have been notified
>
> **Rationale:**
> [Brief explanation - runway extension, refocus, etc.]
>
> **Severance:**
> - [X] weeks pay per year of service
> - [Benefits continuation details]
> - [Other support]
>
> **Financial Impact:**
> - Monthly burn reduction: $[X]
> - New runway: [X] months
> - One-time severance cost: $[X]
>
> **Communication:**
> - Employees: [Notified today via all-hands]
> - Customers: [Plan, if any]
> - Press: [No proactive outreach / statement if asked]
>
> **Team Morale:**
> [Your assessment and plan to support remaining team]
>
> I'm available to discuss. Please don't hesitate to reach out with questions.
>
> [Name]

### Pivot/Strategic Change Notification

**Subject:** [Company] - Strategic Direction Update

> Dear Board,
>
> I want to share a significant strategic decision and the thinking behind it.
>
> **The Decision:**
> We are pivoting from [current direction] to [new direction].
>
> **Background:**
> [What we've learned that led to this]
> - [Key data point 1]
> - [Key data point 2]
> - [Customer/market feedback]
>
> **New Direction:**
> - Target market: [Who]
> - Problem: [What we're solving]
> - Solution: [How]
> - Why we believe: [Evidence/hypothesis]
>
> **What We Preserve:**
> [Technology, relationships, learnings that carry forward]
>
> **Implications:**
> - Timeline: [How long until we can validate]
> - Burn rate: [Impact on financials]
> - Team: [Changes needed, if any]
> - Fundraising: [How this affects next raise]
>
> **My Confidence Level:**
> [Honest assessment of conviction and risk]
>
> I'd like to discuss this at our next board meeting [or request an ad-hoc call]. Please let me know your initial reactions or questions.
>
> [Name]

### Down Round Notification

**Subject:** [Company] - Financing Update

> Dear Board,
>
> I want to update you on our financing situation and the path forward.
>
> **Current Status:**
> We have a term sheet from [investor] at $[X]M pre-money, which is below our last round of $[Y]M.
>
> **How We Got Here:**
> [Brief explanation of market conditions, company performance, etc.]
>
> **Terms Summary:**
> - Pre-money: $[X]M ([Z]% down from last round)
> - Round size: $[X]M
> - Lead investor: [Name]
> - Key terms: [Liquidation preference, anti-dilution, etc.]
>
> **Dilution Impact:**
> [Summary of impact on founders, employees, existing investors]
>
> **Alternative Options Considered:**
> - [Option 1]: [Why not pursuing]
> - [Option 2]: [Why not pursuing]
>
> **My Recommendation:**
> [Accept/negotiate/continue looking] because [reasons]
>
> **What We Need From You:**
> - [Approval to proceed]
> - [Waiver of anti-dilution, if applicable]
> - [Bridge financing while closing]
>
> Please let me know your thoughts. I'd like to [schedule a call / discuss at next board meeting].
>
> [Name]

## Investor Communication

**Subject:** [Company] [Month] Update

> Hi [Name],
>
> Here's our [month] update. I want to be transparent - this was a challenging month.
>
> **TL;DR:**
> [1-2 sentence summary - be honest about challenges]
>
> **Key Metrics:**
> | Metric | This Month | Last Month | Target |
> |--------|------------|------------|--------|
> | [Metric 1] | | | |
> | [Metric 2] | | | |
>
> **Highlights:**
> - [Positive 1]
> - [Positive 2]
>
> **Challenges:**
> - [Challenge 1] - What we're doing: [Action]
> - [Challenge 2] - What we're doing: [Action]
>
> **Runway:**
> [X] months at current burn
>
> **Ask:**
> [Specific ask, if any - intro, advice, etc.]
>
> Happy to chat if you have questions or advice.
>
> [Name]

## Customer Communication

### Service Disruption

**Subject:** Important Update About [Service]

> Dear [Customer Name],
>
> I'm writing to inform you about [issue] that is affecting our service.
>
> **What happened:**
> [Clear, simple explanation]
>
> **What this means for you:**
> [Specific impact on their use of your product]
>
> **What we're doing:**
> [Actions to resolve]
>
> **Timeline:**
> [When you expect resolution]
>
> **What you need to do:**
> [Any actions required on their part, or "No action needed"]
>
> We apologize for the inconvenience. If you have questions, please contact [support email/person].
>
> [Name]
> [Title]

### Product Discontinuation

**Subject:** Important Update About [Product]

> Dear [Customer Name],
>
> I'm writing with important news about [product/feature] that affects your account.
>
> **The Change:**
> We will be discontinuing [product/feature] on [date].
>
> **Why:**
> [Honest, brief explanation]
>
> **What this means for you:**
> [Specific impact]
>
> **Your options:**
> - [Option 1]: [Description]
> - [Option 2]: [Description]
> - [Migration path, if any]
>
> **Timeline:**
> - [Date]: [Milestone]
> - [Date]: Final date for [action]
>
> **Support:**
> We're here to help with this transition. Contact [support] for assistance.
>
> Thank you for being a customer. We're committed to making this transition as smooth as possible.
>
> [Name]

>
> [Name]

## Press/Media

### Basic Holding Statement

> "[Company] is aware of [situation]. We are [looking into it / addressing it / taking it seriously]. We will provide more information as appropriate."

### Layoff Statement (If Asked)

> "Today we made the difficult decision to reduce our team by [X] employees. This decision was not made lightly. We are providing affected employees with [severance summary]. We remain committed to [mission] and are focused on [path forward]."

### Pivot/Strategy Change Statement

> "[Company] is evolving our focus to [new direction]. This builds on what we've learned [serving customers / in the market] and positions us to [value proposition]. We're excited about this direction and grateful to our team and customers for their continued support."

### Down Round Statement (If Disclosed)

> "[Company] has raised $[X] million in new funding. This investment enables us to [plans]. We're grateful for the continued support of our investors as we [mission/goal]."

Note: Generally, avoid discussing valuation publicly. If pressed: "We don't comment on valuation details."

### Negative Press Response

**Only if responding:**

> "Regarding the [article/report] about [topic]: [Specific factual correction if needed]. [What you're doing / your position]. [Forward-looking statement if appropriate]."

**Standard non-response:**

> "We don't comment on [rumor/speculation/personnel matters/ongoing legal matters]."
