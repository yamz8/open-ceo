# Startup Metrics Benchmarks by Stage

## SaaS Benchmarks

### Pre-Seed Stage

**Focus**: Product-market fit signals, not revenue metrics

| Metric | Expectation | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Users/Beta customers | 10-100 | Quality > quantity |
| Engagement | High | Define your activation metric |
| Retention (user) | >40% M1 | Early signal of value |
| Revenue | $0-$10K MRR | Nice to have, not required |
| Burn | $20-50K/mo | Lean team |
| Runway | 18-24 months | Post-raise target |

**What investors look for**:
- Team quality and domain expertise
- Unique insight or unfair advantage
- Early user love (qualitative feedback)
- Large market opportunity

### Seed Stage

**Focus**: Early revenue traction, repeatable sales

| Metric | Minimum | Good | Great |
|--------|---------|------|-------|
| ARR | $100K | $300K | $500K+ |
| MoM Growth | 10% | 15% | 20%+ |
| Customers | 10-20 | 30-50 | 50+ |
| NRR | 90% | 100% | 110%+ |
| Gross Margin | 60% | 70% | 80%+ |
| Burn Multiple | <4x | <3x | <2x |
| Runway | 18 mo | 24 mo | 30 mo |

**What investors look for**:
- Evidence of product-market fit
- Early signs of repeatable sales motion
- Strong retention signals
- Founder-market fit

| Metric | Minimum | Good | Great |

## Red Flags by Stage

### Universal Red Flags

- NRR < 80%
- Logo churn > 5%/month
- Gross margin < 60%
- Burn multiple > 5x
- CAC payback > 36 months
- Concentration > 30% (single customer)

### Stage-Specific Red Flags

**Seed**:
- Zero paying customers after 12 months
- Growth stalled without clear reason
- Burn rate accelerating without corresponding growth

**Series A**:
- Growth decelerating significantly
- NRR trending down
- Sales cycle lengthening
- Churn spiking

**Series B+**:
- Rule of 40 < 20
- Magic number < 0.5
- Churn accelerating with scale
- Unit economics degrading
