# Investor Outreach Templates

## Cold Email Templates

### Template 1: Metric-Led (Best for traction)

**Subject**: [Company] - $[X]K MRR, [X]% MoM growth

```
Hi [First Name],

I'm [Your Name], founder of [Company]. We're [one-sentence description].

Quick stats:
- $[X]K MRR, growing [X]% month-over-month
- [X] customers including [notable logo]
- [Key differentiator or insight]

I'm raising a $[X]M [Seed/Series A] to [primary use of funds]. Given your investments in [relevant portfolio company], I thought [Company] might be interesting.

Would you have 20 minutes this week to chat?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Story-Led (Best for pre-traction)

**Subject**: [Problem statement in customer's words]

```
Hi [First Name],

[X]% of [target customers] struggle with [specific problem]. We lived this at [previous company/experience], where we [specific painful example].

We built [Company] to [solution in one sentence]. Early results:
- [Early signal 1]
- [Early signal 2]

I've followed your writing on [topic] and believe our approach aligns with your thesis on [relevant investment theme].

I'm raising $[X]M to [milestone]. Would love to share what we're building.

20 minutes this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 3: Referral-Led (Best for warm intros)

**Subject**: Intro from [Referrer Name] - [Company]

```
Hi [First Name],

[Referrer] suggested I reach out. We're both [connection point - e.g., alums, worked together, etc.].

I'm building [Company] - [one-sentence description]. [Referrer] thought you'd be interested because [specific reason].

We're at $[X] ARR growing [X]%, and I'm raising a $[X]M [round].

Would love to connect for 20 minutes if you're interested.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 4: Thesis-Led (When investor has published thesis)

**Subject**: [Company] - [Thesis alignment]

```
Hi [First Name],

I read your post on [topic/thesis] and it resonated deeply. You wrote: "[Relevant quote]" - that's exactly what we're building.

[Company] is [description that maps to their thesis].

Early proof points:
- [Metric 1]
- [Metric 2]

I'm raising $[X]M to [goal]. Given your thesis, I think we'd have a great conversation.

15-20 minutes this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Follow-Up Sequences

### Sequence 1: No Response After Initial Email

**Follow-up 1 (Day 3)**
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

```
Hi [First Name],

Wanted to bump this - I know inboxes get busy.

One thing I didn't mention: [new data point, customer win, or insight since last email].

Worth a quick chat?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Follow-up 2 (Day 7)**
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

```
Hi [First Name],

Following up once more. Since my last note:
- [Progress update 1]
- [Progress update 2]

If now isn't the right time, I completely understand. Would it be helpful if I reached back out in [timeframe]?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Follow-up 3 (Day 14) - Final**
Subject: Should I close the loop?

```
Hi [First Name],

I'll assume the timing isn't right if I don't hear back. No hard feelings at all.

If anything changes, you can always reach me at [email]. I'll send a brief update when we hit [next milestone].

All the best,
[Your Name]
```

### Sequence 2: After Initial Meeting (No Decision)

**Post-meeting follow-up (Same day)**
```
Hi [First Name],

Great connecting today. As discussed:
- [Key point 1 from conversation]
- [Key point 2]
- [Action items, if any]

I've attached [deck/materials discussed]. Let me know if you need anything else for your process.

Looking forward to next steps.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Check-in (1 week)**
```
Hi [First Name],

Wanted to check in on timing. Happy to answer any questions that came up in your review or connect you with [customer reference/co-investor/etc.].

On our end: [brief progress update].

What would be helpful for your process?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Warm Introduction Requests

### Asking for an Intro

**To a mutual connection:**
```
Hi [Connector Name],

I'm raising a [Seed/Series A] for [Company] and noticed you're connected to [Investor Name] at [Firm].

Quick context: [2-sentence company description + traction]

Would you be comfortable making an intro? I've drafted a forwardable blurb below if helpful.

---

Hi [Investor First Name],

I wanted to introduce you to [Your Name], founder of [Company]. They're [description].

Key stats: [2-3 bullets]

[Your Name] - over to you to share more.

---

Thanks so much either way!

[Your Name]
```

### Making it Easy to Forward

Always provide a "forwardable blurb" that includes:
1. One-sentence description
2. 2-3 proof points
3. Clear ask
4. Why this investor specifically

## Decline Response

**When an investor passes:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for the update and for considering [Company]. I appreciate the transparency.

A few questions if you have a moment:
- Was there a primary concern or gap?
- Anyone else you'd suggest I speak with?

I'll keep you posted on our progress. Would love to reconnect when we hit [next milestone].

All the best,
[Your Name]
```

## Subject Line Best Practices

**High-performing patterns:**
- `[Company] - $[X]K MRR, [X]% growth`
- `Intro from [Known Name] - [Company]`
- `[Customer problem in their words]`
- `[Company] - [Your thesis alignment]`
- `Quick question about [their portfolio company]`

**Avoid:**
- Generic: "Investment opportunity"
- Too long: Over 50 characters
- Clickbait: "You won't believe this startup"
- No context: Just company name

---

## Email Hygiene Checklist

Before sending, verify:
- [ ] Investor name spelled correctly
- [ ] Firm name correct
- [ ] Relevant portfolio company mentioned
- [ ] Numbers are current and accurate
- [ ] Subject line under 50 characters
- [ ] Email under 150 words
- [ ] Clear ask (meeting request)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting
- [ ] Sent from professional email domain
